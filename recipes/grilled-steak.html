Grilled Steak

Description

A staple American dish.

For best result use NY/KC strip, ribeye, t-bone or tenderloin cuts.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak

Steps

  1. Bring steak to room temperature
  2. Season the steak
  3. Light up the charcoals
  4. Dump the charcoals inside the grill
  5. Throw some wood chips over the charcoals
  6. Grill the steak until done to desired level

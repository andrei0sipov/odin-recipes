Air Fried Salmon

Description

Delicious salmon done in air fryer.

Nutricious and easy to make meal.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Sprinkle lemon juice on the salmon fillets
  2. Season fillets with salt and pepper
  3. Lightly coat with olive oil
  4. Air fry on 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes
